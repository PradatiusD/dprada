Analytics

By installing Google Analytics on your website, we can monitor and track the demographics, locations, operating systems, and profiles of the kinds of customers viewing your webpage. With this data, we can build you reports that not only provide you with raw data, but also analysis on how we can modify your webpage to improve its visibility.

Building SEO

Search Engine Optomization, or SEO, is the task of improving one's rankings on searches for Yahoo!, Bing, and Google. By building the amounts of links that connect back to your webpage, and by developing content, we can work to improve your SEO so that when customers try to find your business through searches, we can build it to your liking. While we can help you improve in all three, most of our experience has been working already with Google Adwords and Google Analytics, for their superior convenience and the fact that it is single-handedly the most visited website in the world.
