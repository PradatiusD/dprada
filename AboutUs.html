Our saga

So what brought such an incredibly charming person onto the glorious path of web design? Unemployment? Boredom? Mide-life crisis? No such thing. We do it because it fills our hearts with joy and wonder, because we love the common humanity of the small entrepreneur, and because we both have a knack for looking smashing.

Daniel Prada

Web Developer/Designer
Daniel was born 13 years after her sister (the oops in the family), and was supposed to have the name "Daniela". Out of love and laziness, his name lost a letter and became his lifelong name. Despite this, Daniel Prada left and became recent graduate of Brown University, where he studied Commerce, Organizations, and Entrepreneurship. He fought diabetes, organized a month of Latino History, and been president of Brown Students for Liberty and the Colombians United at Brown. Outside of his inspiring life, Daniel Prada has been a consistent blogger, writing on three seperate blogs discussing anything to marketing strategies with social media to the relationship between Kid Cudi and our conception of time. Beyond this, Daniel obssesses over ultimate frisbee and the relationship between social justice and libertarianism.
