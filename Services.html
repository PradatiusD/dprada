Web design made a conversation

Web Design is not about installing graphics on a website, it is about creating tone, content, and energy that precisely matches your company culture and your target market. This process is as strategic as any other element of marketing. To begin, we will develop a keyword strategy to ensure that your domain, content, and target those specific audiences that your brand is best for. Using Google’s Keyword Tool, we’ll figure out what 3-5 keywords are most relevant to the customers that search your business. From there we will find the domain, images, and content content that will get you the highest returing search engine presence. What kind of websites do we offer? We offer three main types:


Landing Page

Cause action

Landing pages are web pages where the goal is to cause action (donations, e-mail sign-ups, coupon registrations, ticket sales, special promotions). With few links, simple navigation, and a user-friendly interface, these pages are excellent for pitching a new company idea, for starting a new campaign, or for creating interest for a new initiative. This is a low-cost high value option, and highly recommended for small-scale projects.

WordPress Websites

Professional and affordable

WordPress is a blogging platform that allows you to be able to create, maintain, and set up beautiful websites with minimal knowledge of HTML, CSS, or Javascript thanks to its massive theme database. Using it, we can build a webpage specific to the person who wants a clean design that can be changed without having to contact us. Wordpress is great for a company/ individual searching for a way to have maximum control over the exact content of the webpage as time passes, but wants the elegance in design of custom-made work. Our services would involve registering the site, installing the wordpress platform, desiging the aesthetics as well as the navigation structure. Then we leave you with the keys to access and change your content.

Full-featured Website

Maximum Possibilities

A full-featured website will give you immense choice and space for either you or us to be creative. Given the fact that, unlike WordPress, we can control all aspects of the website code and graphics, we can develop a design solution unique to what you have in mind. Additionally, we'll be able to create our own graphics and also implant them on the site. This is best if you plan on having more complicated content, like galleries, forms, e-commerce platforms, Google Analytics, company e-mails, social media integration, programs, etc.

Figuring out what is best for you

Not every single company needs to focus time, energy, and resources to improving online presence. The two questions you should ask yourself are:

  • How do new consumers find out about my company?
  • How would a user look up your service?

This can help you see what a possible consumer might do, and help you come up with an idea of how we here can help you achieve your marketing goals.

We will start with selecting 3-5 keywords that concern your business and then tailor the coding and content to improve your rankings for those specific accounts.Google Keyword Tool. From here we will try to gear our language, coding, and design as many pathways to bringing in traffic.

Building Social Media

Social Media is a force little understood for most small business owners. But it is crucial as part of a search engine marketing (SEM) campaign to improve your performance on search engines like Bing, Yahoo, Web. After we optimize your webpage content for these search engines, we will proceed to focus first on social media players like Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, Pinterest and others since they are easy, low-cost, high-value ways to improve your website’s SEO performance. Just take Facebook for example: it has more than 800 million monthly active users as of last year, and they all could be interacting with your content. By publish material relevant to your audiences and likely to be shared, you can reach clients outside your client network.

Building engaging content with your social media accounts will not only improve your appearance on search engines, it will also lead to higher traffic, better quality leads, and an audience for your goods or services.

Evaluating your strategy

Without a means to refine and evaluate your marketing strategy, there is no way to know if the marketing strategy have implemented is succeeding or in need of improvement.

Employing Google Analytics

By installing Google Analytics on your website, we can monitor and track the demographics, locations, operating systems, and profiles of the kinds of customers viewing your webpage. With this data, we can build you reports that not only provide you with raw data, but also analysis on how we can modify your webpage to improve its visibility.

As you can see from the sidebar to the right (which is a collage of cutouts from a report on our webpage as of September 21st) you can have precise information on the distribution of operating systems, cities, browsers, internet providers, returning/new, languages, internet service providers, mobile viewers, organic/referral/direct, and so much more. With this wealth of data, we can work with you to understand exactly how your messaging needs to adapt to your audiences.

Integrating your page with Google Adwords
