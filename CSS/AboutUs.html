Daniel Prada is a recent graduate of Brown University, where he studied Commerce, Organizations, and Entrepreneurship. Outside of his design work, Daniel Prada has been a consistent blogger, writing on three seperate blogs discussing anything to marketing strategies with social media to the relationship between Kid Cudi and our conception of time.
