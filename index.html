Loving Day

An event to celebrate interracial Marriage

In 1967 a U.S. Supreme Court ruled bans on interracial marriage unconstitutional, and this site is part of a project here in Miami to celebrate free love.

Our focus centered on mobile-responsive design, utilizing the Foundation 4 framework by ZURB and an instagram feed sharing #lovingday images. The GitHub project of the code is available here and the actual site is here.

The 360 Site

Rebranding for an artist management group.

Our attention centered on finding a theme that visually displayed the amazing breadth and depth of artists they managed and then wrapping it inside an identity that conveyed a classic, elite, and professional attitude towards managing big names in the industry. This project involved an implementation of a Themeforest Theme with image retouching, adding a mobile navigation, and redesigning the identity.

Alain Pupo

Connecting viewers with their clarivoyant

Alain Pupo, a local Miami celibrity clarivoyant, needed a way to connect and manage his web presence to better connect with his viewers.

We built a website around his logo, and took it straight from photoshop to WordPress using the Thesis Framework, adding in Twitter Feeds, MailChimp, Media grids, and (soon) e-commerce sections.

Studio Issa

Adaptive Design

This is a Providence based creative studio that came to me asking for help with their website's performance and appearance on mobile and tablet devices.

Our involvement in the project was to improve the presentation of the site at mobile, tablet, and desktop resolutions.

College Unwrapped

Stories from Ivy League college students

Often prestigious institutions aren't in the mindset of low-income students because they never interact with these peoples. Our work focused on taking these visual narratives and finding a shareable means.

This project involved modifying a Themeforest theme and adding the ability for the homepage images to have individual and dynamically generated twitter/fb buttons that shared the title and description.
