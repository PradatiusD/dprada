Branding

A logo to many is just a fancy picture to put on a website. But in reality, brand identities are crucial visual cues of repeatable quality. For example, would you buy a Macbook Pro on Amazon that, instead of an apple behind the screen, had a banana? Would you eat a McDonalds burger on the other side of the world if it weren't wrapped, presented, and decorated as in your hometown? No. Why? Because you associate those logos, fonts, colors, images, and ideas with those logos. For this reason, I spend hours thoughtfully creating and building an identity that is both authentic and reasoned.
